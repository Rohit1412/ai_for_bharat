## 💡 KEY MESSAGES FOR JUDGES

1. **Use AWS Bedrock**: This MVP directly uses Claude 3.5 Sonnet via Bedrock for action plan optimization + RAG chat. Not a mock. Real, live inference.
2. **Scalability**: Serverless Lambda + DynamoDB on pay-per-request means zero infrastructure overhead. Works for 10 farmers or 100,000 alike.
3. **Practicality**: Every intervention shown has real subsidy links, ROI numbers, and implementation timelines. Farmers can act immediately, not in 6 months.
4. **Bilingual**: Kannada support is native, not bolted-on. Judges will see responses in farmers' own language.
5. **Real Data**: Maps use actual satellite imagery (Sentinel-5P, Landsat). KPIs map to real greenhouse gas and water databases.
